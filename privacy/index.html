Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

LetterForge ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we handle when you use letterforge.com or our mobile app.

Summary in one line: Your data is processed securely and never sold.

What we process

  • Job description text you paste into the generator
  • Resume or background text you provide as input
  • Generated cover letters produced for your session
  • Payment metadata (transaction ID, plan tier) if you upgrade — handled by Stripe or our MRV payment partner
  • Basic analytics (page views, anonymous usage counts) to understand product use

What we do not collect

  • We do not require an account or login for the free tier
  • We do not collect phone numbers
  • We do not store your job descriptions or resumes after your generation session ends
  • We do not sell any data to third parties — ever

How your data is used

Job description and resume text are sent to a large language model (OpenAI / Anthropic API) to generate a cover letter tailored to that role. The text is processed in transit and is not retained by us after the response is returned.

Payment information is processed by Stripe (stripe.com/privacy) or by our MRV wallet partner. We never see or store your full payment card.

Data retention

Session inputs are discarded as soon as your cover letter is generated. Payment receipts are retained for tax/accounting purposes (typically 7 years, as required by law).

Your rights

You can request deletion of any data we hold about you by emailing the address below. We respond within 30 days.

Security

Traffic is served over HTTPS. Inputs sent to upstream AI providers travel over encrypted connections. We follow industry-standard practices to protect our infrastructure.

Children

LetterForge is intended for adults seeking employment. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

Changes

If we materially update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify users via the app.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests: contact@morvenempire.com
