FAQ

Questions answered

Do I need an API key?

Yes — you need a free Anthropic API key to generate letters. Get one at console.anthropic.com. New accounts get free credits that cover dozens of letters. Your key is stored only in your browser and never sent to our servers.

Is my data private?

Your job descriptions and background text are sent directly to Anthropic's API from your browser. We don't store, log, or see any of your data. Everything is processed client-side.

How is this better than ChatGPT?

LetterForge uses a carefully engineered prompt that mirrors the company's language, maps your experience to their exact requirements, and avoids the generic phrases that ATS systems flag. ChatGPT with no instructions gives you a template — we give you a strategy.

Can I use it for any job?

Absolutely. Tech, finance, design, marketing, healthcare — the AI adapts to any industry and role level. Works best with full job descriptions rather than just a title.
